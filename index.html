What is acupuncture?

Acupuncture is a natural way to get well and stay well

Acupuncture is an ancient form of medicine with increasing scientific research supporting its beneficial use.

The following conditions have been clinically proven to respond well to Acupuncture:

  • Short-term relief of tension type headaches
  • Short-term relief of migraine headache
  • Short-term relief of chronic low back pain
  • Short-term relief of neck pain
  • Short-term relief from temporomandibular pain
  • Temporary adjunctive treatment for osteoarthritis knee pain

Acupuncture concentrates on helping the body healing itself by using points unique to you, specific to what you need.

Acupuncture treats the root cause and not just the symptoms.

In illness, energy flow can become blocked or disrupted causing pain and disease. Acupuncture works by re-establishing the body's normal flow of energy.
